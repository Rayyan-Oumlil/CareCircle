# Limites des Études de Référence

## Étude MH-TIPS - Limites

**Source** : [School Nurses' Experiences in Dealing with Adolescents Having Mental Health Problems](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9449503/)

### Limites identifiées

> "The results of this study represent findings from identified school nurse experts and a national convenience sample of practicing school nurses attending a NASN Annual Conference. While both school nurse experts and focus group participants represented a diverse group of school nurses, it is possible that these findings may not generalize to all school nurses. In addition, purposive sampling was not used for key informant interviews, but data revealed a variety of perspectives presented. Moreover, some indicators of saturation were present related to repeating information among interviews, as well as consistencies in interview and focus group data, particularly for concepts of training features and barriers to mental health practice. Finally, demographic and other descriptive characteristics of the participants are minimal and could be expanded upon in future studies. The purpose of this study was exploratory and to inform MH-TIPS program development. Future research evaluating MH-TIPS feasibility, acceptability, and school nurse and student outcomes after participation in the MH-TIPS is necessary."

### Points clés pour notre projet

1. **Généralisation limitée** : Résultats peuvent ne pas s'appliquer à tous les infirmières scolaires
2. **Échantillonnage** : Convenience sample, pas échantillonnage probabiliste
3. **Caractéristiques démographiques** : Minimales, pourraient être élargies
4. **Recherche future nécessaire** : Évaluation de faisabilité, acceptabilité, et résultats

### Pourquoi notre solution est différente

- **MH-TIPS** : Favorise la formation continue (approche traditionnelle)
- **CareCircle** : Système d'agents IA spécialisés (approche innovante)
- **Complémentaire** : Notre solution n'existe pas encore et offre une alternative
